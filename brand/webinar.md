# Webinar Script — 90-Minute Framework

**Title**: "How One Person Runs a 13-Person Marketing Operation From Their Phone"
**Goal**: Convert $97 buyers into $597 buyers. Qualify leads for $5K-$18K strategy call.
**Format**: Live (high-ticket requires live). Brunson Perfect Webinar framework.
**CTA**: "Apply for a Strategy Call" (not "Buy Now")

---

## [0:00–0:05] Pre-Show

- Music, early-bird chat
- Slide: "Starting in 5 minutes — close your other tabs"
- Poll: "How many AI subscriptions are you currently paying for?"

## [0:05–0:15] Introduction

### Big Promise
"In the next 90 minutes, I'm going to show you the exact system I use to run an entire marketing operation — video editing, ad copy, social content, email campaigns, transcription — from my phone. Without an agency. Without freelancers. Without a single cloud AI subscription."

### Hook to End
"If you stay to the end, I'll give you the complete Ghost Agency blueprint — the exact setup I use, the tools, the commands, the workflow. Plus access to a private strategy call where we map this to your business."

### Command Attention
"Close your email. Close your Slack. This is the last 90 minutes you'll spend leashed to a dashboard."

### Qualify Self (Epiphany Bridge — Origin Story)
John's story:
- Built a $500K/month affiliate business (Forbes, Entrepreneur, NBC)
- Was spending $200/month on AI subscriptions + feeding client data to cloud servers
- The moment of realization: "I'm training my competitors' AI with my own proprietary work"
- Spent a year building the alternative: a terminal that runs on his own machine
- Named it Bonfire

### Future Pace
"Imagine waking up tomorrow. You send a voice note from your phone while making coffee. By the time you sit down, the video is edited, the blog post is drafted, and 3 social posts are queued. No agency invoice. No freelancer Slack messages. No dashboard to check. Just output. From your machine."

---

## [0:15–0:32] Secret 1: The Vehicle (Breaking the "wrong tool" belief)

### False belief to break
"Cloud AI tools are the best way to use AI in my business."

### Epiphany Bridge Story
The subscription trap: started with ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo), then Claude ($20), then Otter ($30), then Midjourney ($30), then API costs ($80+). $200/month. $2,400/year. Every prompt going to someone else's server.

Then: LinkedIn auto-enrolled 1B users into AI training (Sept 2025). X/Twitter claimed perpetual rights to all AI outputs (Dec 2025). 225,000+ ChatGPT credentials found on dark web.

The realization: **cloud AI is rented infrastructure that trains on your work and charges you for the privilege.**

### The New Opportunity
Local AI. Everything runs on your machine. Your phone is the remote control. Your desktop is the engine room. You own the tool. You own the data. You own the output.

### Framework
The One-Person Empire model:
- 13 outsourced roles → 1 person + local AI terminal
- Phone-first commands (Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp, Slack)
- 16 bundled tools (FFmpeg, Whisper, TinyLlama)
- $5,000 once vs $2,400/year forever (breakeven: 25 months, then free)

### Proof
- Uber burned its 2026 AI budget in 4 months on cloud AI tools
- Microsoft canceled Claude Code licenses by June 2026
- Freelancer marketplace spend collapsed 79% since 2021
- AI replaces freelancers at 33:1 cost ratio (Ramp data)

## [0:32–0:47] Secret 2: Internal Belief (Breaking "I can't do this")

### False belief to break
"I'm not technical enough to run local AI."

### Epiphany Bridge Story
Show a non-technical user (marketer/coach) using Bonfire. The entire interaction is: send a Telegram message, get a result back. That's it. No terminal commands. No Python. No Docker.

### Live Demo
- Voice note → transcription (Whisper, local)
- Image → edited with overlay (FFmpeg, local)
- Text command → AI response (TinyLlama or Claude API)
- All from phone, all processed on desktop in background

### Key message
"If you can send a text message, you can run a one-person empire. The terminal handles the hard part. You handle the decisions."

---

## [0:47–0:60] Secret 3: External Belief (Breaking "something will stop me")

### False belief to break
"I don't have time / the market is too competitive / AI changes too fast."

### The Time Reframe
"How many hours per week do you spend in dashboards? Managing freelancers? Switching between tools? That time IS the cost of not having a terminal. Bonfire doesn't add to your schedule. It replaces what's already consuming it."

### The Competition Reframe
"Your competitors are still paying $16,500/month for outsourced teams. You'll be running the same output from your phone for $5,000 total. That's not a competitive advantage — that's a competitive moat."

### The AI Change Reframe
"Bonfire is provider agnostic. When the next model comes out, you switch. No migration. No new subscription. No vendor lock-in. The terminal stays the same. The AI inside it evolves."

### Data Points
- Average business wastes $7,900-$17,000/employee/year on unused SaaS (47% of licenses unused)
- EU AI Act fines: up to 7% of global revenue (enforcement Aug 2026)
- 294 OpenAI outages since Jan 2025 (61 hours of downtime/year)
- The entrepreneurs who moved first already have the head start
