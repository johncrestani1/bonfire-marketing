# Positioning

## The single sentence

**Bonfire Terminal is the local AI terminal that runs your entire marketing operation from your own machine.**

## For whom

Marketers, content creators, and coaches who are currently:
- Paying $10,000-37,000/month for outsourced teams, OR
- Burning out doing everything themselves, OR
- Spending $200+/month on cloud AI subscriptions that harvest their data

## Against whom

The primary competitor is not software. It is **the outsourced agency** — the $10K-37K/month team of video editors, copywriters, social media managers, ad specialists, and project coordinators that entrepreneurs hire because they can't do it all alone.

Bonfire doesn't compete with ChatGPT. Bonfire competes with **hiring people**.

## How we win

| Their world | Our world |
|---|---|
| 13 outsourced roles, $16,500-37,000/month | 1 person + Bonfire, $5,000 once |
| Your data on their cloud servers | Your data on your machine |
| Monthly subscriptions that stack | One-time investment, you own it |
| Vendor lock-in, price increases, ToS changes | Runs locally, no dependency |
| Learning 8 different tools and dashboards | One terminal, commands via your phone |

## What we are NOT

**Not a chatbot.** Bonfire is not another ChatGPT wrapper. It's an operations terminal that executes work — video editing, transcription, ad copy, content production — not a conversation interface.

**Not a SaaS subscription.** $5,000 one-time. You own it. It runs on your hardware. No recurring charges, no "free tier" bait-and-switch, no vendor holding your workflow hostage.

## The positioning statement

For **marketers, content creators, and coaches** who need to produce at the scale of a full agency, **Bonfire Terminal** is the **local AI terminal** that **replaces your outsourced team with AI running on your own machine**. Unlike **cloud AI tools and automation platforms**, Bonfire **runs entirely on your hardware, costs $5,000 once, and puts you in command of a 13-role operation through your phone's messaging apps**.
