## PRODUCTION NOTES

1. **One person produces all of this.** Every idea is designed to be executed by one person with a camera, a screen recorder, and Bonfire Terminal. No production team required. This IS the Ghost Agency model in practice.

2. **Batch production works.** Group by format: record all YouTube talking heads in one session, write all Twitter threads in one sitting, film all short-form hooks in one afternoon.

3. **Repurpose aggressively.** Every YouTube video contains at least one Twitter thread, one short-form clip, one blog post outline, and one email newsletter angle. The 100 ideas here are 100 starting points, not 100 isolated pieces.

4. **Data is the differentiator.** Every hot take and provocation is backed by real data from the manifesto evidence file. This is not opinion content -- it is data-driven argument content. Always cite sources.

5. **The enemy makes the content.** Dashboard Slavery is the through-line. Even tutorials should open with the pain point (too many tools, too many tabs, too much subscription spend) before showing the solution.
