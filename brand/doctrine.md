# The Bonfire Doctrine

Five immutable laws. Everything we build, sell, and say derives from these.

---

## Doctrine 1: AI Amplifies Entrepreneurs

**"AI is the workforce. You're the boss."**

AI does not replace the entrepreneur. AI replaces the 13 people the entrepreneur was paying to execute. The entrepreneur directs. AI executes. The human is always in command.

This is not a philosophical position. It is an architectural decision. Bonfire Terminal is built so the human issues commands and the AI produces output. There is no "autonomous agent" mode. There is no "let the AI decide." You decide. The terminal executes.

---

## Doctrine 2: Phone-First Command, Computer-Second Execution

**"Your phone is the remote. Your desktop is the engine."**

You should not be leashed to a laptop to run your business. Commands flow from your phone — through Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp, Slack — to the terminal running on your desktop. The AI processes locally. The result comes back to your phone.

You command from wherever you are. The engine room runs itself.

---

## Doctrine 3: Provider Agnostic

**"No single vendor owns your future."**

Bonfire connects to local models (TinyLlama), OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, and Grok. You choose. You switch. You pay only for what you use, when you use it.

When OpenAI raised prices from $20 to $200/month — and admitted they still lose money at that price — Bonfire users shrugged. They were running TinyLlama locally. When Microsoft canceled Claude Code licenses after burning through their AI budget in 4 months, Bonfire users were unaffected. No single vendor controls their workflow.

Provider agnosticism is not a feature. It is insurance.

---

## Doctrine 4: Your Machine, Your Data, Your Rules

**"If it leaves your device, it's not yours anymore."**

LinkedIn opted 1 billion users into AI training by default in September 2025 and is now facing a class-action lawsuit. X/Twitter rewrote its terms in December 2025 to claim perpetual, royalty-free rights to every AI prompt and output — with no opt-out. 225,000+ ChatGPT credentials have been found on dark web markets.

These are not hypothetical risks. They are Tuesday.

Bonfire Terminal runs on your machine. Your prompts stay on your machine. Your client data stays on your machine. Nothing leaves unless you explicitly choose a cloud provider for a specific task — and even then, the orchestration is local.

Ownership is not a setting you toggle. It is the architecture.

---

## Doctrine 5: Entrepreneurs Manage AI Workforces

**"You're the general. AI is the army."**

The next generation of businesses will not hire 13 people. They will hire one person and give them a terminal.

Freelancer marketplace spending has collapsed 79% since 2021. Freelance writing is down 30%, design down 17%, development down 21%. AI replaces freelancers at a 33:1 cost ratio. This is not a prediction. This is Ramp's transaction data.

The question is not whether AI will replace outsourced teams. The question is whether YOU will be the one managing the AI workforce — or whether you'll be one of the 13 people getting replaced.

Bonfire Terminal is the command center for the general who chose to lead.
