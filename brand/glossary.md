# Bonfire Terminal Glossary

Standard language. Use these terms consistently. If engineering, sales, affiliates, and customers all use different words for the same thing, the market gets confused.

## Core Terms

| Term | Definition | Use it when... | Don't say... |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bonfire Terminal** | The product. Always two words, always capitalized. | Referring to the software | "Bonfire app," "the Bonfire," "BT" |
| **Local AI terminal** | The category. | Describing what Bonfire IS | "AI tool," "AI platform," "AI software," "chatbot" |
| **Terminal** | The command center on your desktop. | Describing the desktop application | "App," "dashboard," "interface" |
| **Bridge** | The connection between your messaging app and the terminal. | Describing Telegram/Discord/WhatsApp/Slack integration | "Integration," "plugin," "connector" |
| **Daemon** | The AI engine running in the background on your machine. | Technical contexts only | Don't use in marketing — say "the AI engine" or "the terminal" |
| **Ghost Agency** | An AI-powered operation that looks like a full team to the outside world. | Describing the business model Bonfire enables | "Automation," "AI business" |
| **Command** | What you send to Bonfire through your messaging app. | Describing user interaction | "Prompt," "query," "request" |
| **Local** | Running on your machine, not in the cloud. | Emphasizing privacy and ownership | "Offline" (Bonfire can use cloud APIs optionally) |
