# Bonfire Terminal — Talking Points

## For podcast hosts, interviewers, and press

### 1. The Local AI Thesis
"Every AI tool today sends your data to someone else's server. Bonfire runs on YOUR machine. Your prompts, your images, your voice — none of it leaves your device. That's not a feature, it's the architecture."

### 2. Why Content Creators Need This
"Creators are the heaviest AI users and the most exposed. They're feeding proprietary content, brand voices, and client data into cloud APIs every day. Bonfire gives them the same capabilities without the data risk."

### 3. The Bridge Architecture
"You don't need to learn a new app. Bonfire works through Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp — the apps you already use. Send a voice note from your phone, get a transcription on your desktop. It's AI that meets you where you are."

### 4. Building Real Software
"We ship a 2GB installer with 16 bundled tools, a Rust daemon, and 35 quality gates. This isn't a wrapper around ChatGPT. It's a compiled, signed, deterministic build that runs on machines from 2013 onwards."

### 5. The Affiliate Model
"Instead of spending on ads, we give creators the tools to promote Bonfire themselves. Pre-built landing pages, banner ads, email templates — everything they need to earn by sharing something they actually use."

### 6. AI Regulation Isn't a Problem If You Run It Locally
"When the model runs on your machine and the output never touches a server, most AI regulation doesn't apply. You own the input, you own the output, you own the process."

### 7. What's Next
"Multi-modal AI is the next frontier — combining text, image, audio, and video in a single pipeline. Bonfire's plugin architecture is built for this. We're also working on protocol-based content rights so creators can prove provenance of AI-assisted work."
