# Bonfire Terminal — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

Bonfire Terminal is a local-first AI harness for content creators. The brand communicates: warmth, power, independence, transparency.

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
The full-color Bonfire flame mark. Use on white or neutral backgrounds.
- **Minimum size**: 32px height for digital, 10mm for print
- **Clear space**: Maintain at least 50% of the logo height as padding on all sides
- **Never**: Stretch, rotate, add effects, change colors, or place on busy backgrounds

### Light Variant
White flame mark for use on dark backgrounds.

### Dark Variant
Dark/black flame mark for use on light backgrounds.

### Icon Only
The flame mark without wordmark. Use for favicons, app icons, and small-space applications.

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Inter** — Used for all UI text, headings, and body copy.
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)
- Source: [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)

### Monospace Font
**JetBrains Mono** — Used for code, terminal output, and technical content.
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 700 (Bold)
- Source: [JetBrains](https://www.jetbrains.com/lp/mono/)

## Voice & Tone

- **Direct**: Say what you mean. No marketing fluff.
- **Technical but accessible**: Use proper terms but explain them.
- **Confident**: We build real software that ships.
- **Transparent**: Local-first, open architecture, no hidden data collection.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use the logo at the correct aspect ratio
- Maintain clear space around the logo
- Use brand colors consistently
- Credit "Bonfire Terminal" (not "Bonfire" alone in ambiguous contexts)

### Don't
- Alter the logo colors or proportions
- Use the logo as part of a sentence
- Combine with other logos without clear separation
- Imply official endorsement without partnership agreement
