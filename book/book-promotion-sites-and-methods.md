## 1. Distribution Model Overview

### How It Works

1. **You get the PPTX source file** from the Bonfire Marketing repo or the affiliate portal.
2. **Customize it:** Change the author name, add your Digistore24/Clickbank affiliate link for Bonfire Terminal anywhere the CTA appears (currently "bonfireterminal.com/apply").
3. **Export to PDF** from PowerPoint, Google Slides, or LibreOffice.
4. **Distribute anywhere** — Gumroad, your website, email list, social media, ebook stores, etc.
5. **Monetize:** When prospects read the book and visit your affiliate link, you earn commission on Bonfire Terminal sales through Digistore24 or Clickbank.

### Two Ways Affiliates Earn

- **Inside the book:** Replace the CTA URLs with your affiliate hop link. Every reader who clicks buys through your link.
- **Follow-up sequences:** Give the book away free, collect the prospect's email/DM, then send your Bonfire Terminal affiliate link in your follow-up messages.

### Affiliate Links Setup

- **Digistore24:** Sign up at https://www.digistore24.com → Search marketplace for "Bonfire Terminal" → Generate your hop link
- **Clickbank:** Sign up at https://accounts.clickbank.com → Search marketplace for "Bonfire Terminal" → Generate your hop link

## 3. SEO & Document-Sharing Sites

Upload the PDF to these high-authority sites for SEO backlinks and organic discoverability. All are free.

### Internet Archive (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

- **URL:** https://archive.org
- **DA:** 93+ | **Monthly visits:** 220M
- **Direct download:** Yes — every file gets a direct download URL
- **Permanent:** Files are archived permanently

**Step-by-step:**
1. Go to https://archive.org/account/signup — create a free account
2. Click the **upload icon** (up arrow) in the top-right corner, or go to https://archive.org/upload
3. Drag and drop your PDF
4. Fill in metadata:
   - **Title:** The Untold Story of Computers
   - **Creator:** [Your Name]
   - **Date:** 2026
   - **Description:** A free 87-page book on the history of computers and why AI is the biggest income opportunity of our generation.
   - **Subject Tags:** AI, artificial intelligence, business, computers, technology, software ownership
   - **Media Type:** texts
5. Click **"Upload and Create Your Item"**
6. Your direct download link will be: `https://archive.org/download/[item-id]/[filename].pdf`

### Scribd

- **URL:** https://www.scribd.com
- **DA:** 93 | **Monthly visits:** 327M
- **Direct download:** No (readers need Scribd subscription to download, but can read online)
- **SEO value:** Excellent — 62% of traffic from Google organic

**Step-by-step:**
1. Go to https://www.scribd.com/signup — create a free account
2. Click **"Upload"** in the top navigation
3. Drag and drop your PDF
4. Fill in:
   - **Title:** The Untold Story of Computers
   - **Description:** Include keywords — "free AI book," "business transformation," "software ownership"
   - **Category:** Technology, Business
5. Click **"Save & Continue"**
6. Document appears publicly and is indexed by Google within 24-72 hours

### SlideShare

- **URL:** https://www.slideshare.net
- **DA:** ~95 | **Monthly visits:** 60M

## 6. Social Media Promotion Playbook

### YouTube

**Best video types:**
- "5 Key Insights From The Untold Story of Computers" (3-5 min)
- Chapter summary deep-dives (7-15 min)
- "Why I Wrote This Book" behind-the-scenes (5-10 min)

**How to link:** First line of video description: "Download the free PDF: [your link]". Pin a comment with the link. Add end-screen card pointing to your landing page.

**Affiliates:** Record your own review or "5 things I learned" video. Link your Gumroad listing or landing page in the description with your affiliate link.

### TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts

**The "Comment BOOK" Strategy (highest conversion):**
1. Record a 15-60 second video: "This 87-page book about AI changed how I think about business — comment BOOK and I'll DM you the free PDF"
2. Set up **ManyChat** (https://manychat.com, free tier available) with keyword trigger on "BOOK"
3. When someone comments "BOOK," ManyChat auto-DMs the download link
4. **Critical:** Set up ManyChat BEFORE posting the video

**Hashtags:** #BookTok #FreeBook #AIBusiness #Entrepreneur #ArtificialIntelligence #BusinessOwner #FreeEbook #TechBooks

**Post frequency:**
- TikTok: 1-2x per day
- Instagram Reels: 4-7x per week
- YouTube Shorts: 3-5x per week

**Expected reach:** 200-100K+ views per video. A video with 10K views might generate 200-500 comments and 100-300 email captures.

### LinkedIn

**PDF Carousel Posts (596% more engagement than text posts):**
1. Create a 7-10 slide PDF summarizing key book insights (one concept per slide)
2. Upload as a document post on LinkedIn
3. Each swipe counts as dwell time — the algorithm rewards this heavily
4. Final slide: "Want the full 87-page book? Comment 'SEND' or click the link in comments."
5. Post your download link in the FIRST COMMENT (not the post body — avoids algorithm penalty on external links)

**LinkedIn Newsletter:**
- 25-35% open rates (vs 21% for traditional email)
- Serialize book content: one chapter summary per edition
- Link to full PDF download in each issue

**LinkedIn Articles:**
- Write thought-leadership pieces expanding on book themes
- Articles rank in Google search and get recommended for weeks
- Include download link at the end of each article

### Twitter/X

**Thread Strategy (3.1x more saves than single tweets):**
1. Tweet 1 (hook): "I just read an 87-page book about AI that every business owner should read. It's free. Here are 7 things that changed how I think about software ownership:"
2. Tweets 2-7: One concrete insight per tweet with standalone value
3. Tweet 8 (CTA): "Want the full 87-page PDF free? Reply 'BOOK' and I'll DM it. Or grab it here: [link]"
4. Tweet 9: "If this was useful, RT tweet 1 so others can see it too."

**Pin** a tweet with the book's strongest hook + download link. Every profile visitor sees it.

**Twitter Spaces:** Schedule weekly 30-60 minute live discussions on "AI and Business Ownership." Mention the book and share the link.

### Facebook

**Groups Strategy (organic, free):**
- Target groups: "AI for Business Owners," "Entrepreneur Mindset," "Small Business Automation," "Digital Marketing"
- Post format: Answer a question or share a framework from the book → "I actually wrote a free 87-page book on this — DM me or comment 'BOOK' for the PDF"
- Avoid direct links in group posts (many groups auto-remove external links)
- Expected: 250-500 leads/month with consistent posting across multiple groups

**Facebook Ads (if budget allows later):**
- Lead Form Ads: $5-$15 CPL for a free book offer
- Video ads: 60% more engagement than image ads
- A 30-second video with the book's key promise + "Download free" CTA

### Podcast Guesting

**How to pitch:**
- Subject: "Guest pitch: The AI ownership problem most business owners don't see coming"
- Body: 3 sentences about who you are + 2-3 specific topics you can discuss + mention the free book as a resource for listeners
- Pitch 3-6 months ahead. Most shows book 4-8 weeks out.

**Booking platforms:**
- **PodMatch** (https://podmatch.com) — ~70% booking rate
- **Matchmaker.FM** (https://matchmaker.fm) — free matching
- **Talks.co** (https://talks.co) — discovery platform

**Target:** Mid-tier podcasts (5,000-50,000 downloads/episode). Large enough audience, small enough to actively seek guests.

### Reddit / Quora

**Reddit:**
- **r/FreeEBOOKS** (~1.2M members) — post the book directly. This subreddit exists for sharing free ebooks.
- **r/Entrepreneur** (~3.5M members), **r/artificial** (~900K), **r/smallbusiness** — participate genuinely, mention the book organically after building karma
- **Rule of thumb:** 90% value contributions, 10% book mentions. Build 500+ karma first.
- **AI citation bonus:** Reddit content gets ~4x more citations from ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity

**Quora:**
- Find high-traffic questions: "What will AI do to small businesses?" / "Best free books about AI?"
- Write detailed 300-500 word answers with genuine value
- Mention the book near the end: "I cover this in depth in a free 87-page book — [link]"
- Quora answers rank in Google and get cited by AI tools
