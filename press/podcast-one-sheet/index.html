Mission & Overview

Mission: I show business owners why owning their AI stack is the biggest leverage play of this decade — and give them the tools to do it.

I started my first online business in 2012 after two years at a marketing agency. That business did seven figures every year since 2014, all without employees. I sold over 20,000 copies of my books through TV infomercials and built one of the largest affiliate marketing education platforms online. In 2024 I pivoted to AI infrastructure. My company, M3M3TIC LLC, builds Bonfire Terminal — a local-first AI platform that lets business owners own their AI tools with a one-time perpetual license instead of paying $1,000+ per month in SaaS subscriptions. My new book, The Untold Story of Computers, lays out the data: the $638 billion AI market, what 2,400 CEOs told BCG, and why ownership beats rental.
